About Us

Tribal Districts Resource Management and Community Development (TDRMCD) is a platform focused on empowering communities in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa through sustainable resource management and collaboration.

Our Mission

To document resources, facilitate knowledge sharing, and support community-led development initiatives across tribal districts.

What We Do
  • Curate and share resource information
  • Host community discussions and campaigns
  • Provide tools for collaboration and learning
